Summary: Latitude vs. X

This webpage is to outline weather patterns across 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. Using data from other resources, we will plot datapounts looking at weather and latitudes. Other factors that will be considered includes cloudiness, wind speeds, and humidity. Finally, we will provide final analysis and reflection on any strong correlations.
